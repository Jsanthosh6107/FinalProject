Gastrointestinal

The destruction of the Gastrointestinal system

Content

The intestines disintegrate rapidly. The gut lining collapses, leading to unstoppable vomiting, bloody diarrhea, and unbearable pain. Dehydration and starvation set in as the body shuts down nutrient absorption entirely.

Bacteria flood the bloodstream, triggering rampant infection and sepsis. The intestines bleed uncontrollably, and organ failure follows. The body is failing, and there is nothing that can be done.
