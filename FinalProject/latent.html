Latent

The quiet before the storm

Content
Stage Three

After the prodromal stage, patients recover almost out of nowhere. To the unknowning, they've undergone a miracle and recovered completely, but to doctors, they're a walking ghost. for around 5 days, they'll feel relatively healthy and energized again, but this is only an intermission to their grueling death.

Beneath the surface, critical systems like the immune and digestive systems are silently failing. Though the patient may feel well and appear normal, this brief period of calm hides the irreversible damage happening inside their body, a quiet prelude to the horrors yet to come.

The latent period can range from 1-5 days depending on your dose. If you had anything left in life to do, this would be your last chance.
