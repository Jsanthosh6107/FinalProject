Hematopoietic

The failing of the Hematopoietic system

Content
Stage Five

Bone marrow is obliterated at this point, erasing the body's ability to produce blood cells. This loss triggers rapid anemia, crippling immune collapse, and uncontrollable bleeding, setting the stage for an agonizing deterioration of the human body.

The absence of blood cell regeneration leaves the body defenseless, while uncontrolled internal bleeding drowns vital organs. With no way to heal, sepsis, hemorrhage, and organ failure set in.
