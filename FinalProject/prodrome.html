The initial phase comes with symptoms such as nausea, vomiting, fatigue, and skin reddening. Depending on how bad your initial symptoms are, this will be your only indication that you've recieved a lethal dose. Sometimes it is accomidated with a migrane, and these symptoms will last for a few hours after exposure.

This phase is used by doctors to determine the dosage of radiation you recieved. Depending on your symptoms, they'll have to decide if it's worth treating you or not. If they decide to, aggressive care is often needed in the earliest stages, though even then, your chances of survival can be minimal.
