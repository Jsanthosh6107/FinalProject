Manifest

The beginning of the end

Content
Stage Four

The manifest phase marks the complete and systematic failure of the body. As organ systems begin to shut down, the symptoms will enter their worst phase. The entire body is plagued with issues as internal damage reaches a critical point.

System Affected Key Symptoms and Effects Details/Explanation
Gastrointestinal (GI) Severe diarrhea, vomiting, abdominal pain, bleeding Damage to the intestines causes dehydration, nutrient loss, and bleeding.
Hematopoietic Low blood cell counts, infections, uncontrolled bleeding Bone marrow failure leads to weak immunity and blood clotting problems.
Neurological Confusion, seizures, loss of coordination, unconsciousness Severe damage to the brain or imbalances in the body affect nerve function.
Skin Skin peeling, ulcers, dead tissue Radiation causes burns and wounds that don’t heal, risking infections.
Cardiovascular Low blood pressure, irregular heartbeat, vascular damage Damage to blood vessels and heart weakens blood circulation.
Respiratory Breathing problems, fluid in the lungs Inflammation or damage to the lungs makes breathing difficult.
Systemic High fever, extreme tiredness, organ failure The body shuts down due to widespread damage and infections.
